Seasons

Spring

Spring is a time when the weather gets warmer and the days become longer.
Flowers start to bloom, trees grow new leaves, and animals come out of hiding.
It is a season of fresh starts and new growth, making everything look beautiful and full of life.

Summer

Summer is known for its hot weather, long days, and sunny skies.
It is a season of outdoor activities, vacations, and relaxation.
People often enjoy swimming and spending time at the beach during summer.
